The Story of Theraseek

At Theraseek, we believe that mental health support should be a universal right, not a luxury. For too long, the journey to finding empathetic, affordable, and accessible therapy has been hindered by long waitlists, high costs, and logistical barriers.

That's why we built Luna, our advanced AI companion. Luna was designed to bridge the gap, providing a safe, judgment-free space for you to unpack your thoughts, day or night. While she doesn't replace the profound impact of professional human therapists, she is here to hold space for you when you need it most.

Our mission is simple: to democratize mental wellness and ensure that no one ever has to face their darkest moments alone.
