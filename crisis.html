Immediate Help & Resources

If you are in immediate danger, please call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Theraseek is an AI companion and is not a substitute for professional mental health care or emergency services. If you are experiencing a crisis, please use the resources below.

US National Resources

  • Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (Available 24/7, Free, Confidential)
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor
  • The Trevor Project (LGBTQ Youth): Call 866-488-7386 or text START to 678-678
  • Veterans Crisis Line: Dial 988, then press 1, or text 838255

International Resources

If you are outside the United States, please reach out to your local emergency services or find a crisis line in your country through Find A Helpline.
