Your privacy

What you tell Seeker
stays with you.

We didn’t want to write another privacy policy and hope you trust it. So we built a system that physically cannot betray you — no policy required, because the architecture doesn’t allow it.

01
Your voice never leaves your phone

Speech-to-text and text-to-speech run entirely on-device. Your audio is never sent to a server — ours or anyone else’s.

02
No cloud AI processing

Seeker runs a self-hosted, fine-tuned model locally on your device. There are no calls to OpenAI, no third-party APIs, no server-side logs.

03
We can’t read your data

If you choose to back up your conversations, they’re zero-knowledge encrypted. Even we cannot decrypt them. This is technical, not just policy.

04
Memory stays on your device

Seeker’s understanding of you is stored encrypted on your phone, not in the cloud. Delete the app and it’s gone — no trace, no backup we’re holding.

“Other apps promise privacy in a document. We built a system that makes betraying you technically impossible.”

Meet Seeker

You don’t always need a therapist. Sometimes you just need someone to actually listen.

Seeker is a voice-first AI companion built for those moments when things feel too heavy to carry alone — but you’re not ready (or able) to call someone. No appointments. No waitlists. No script. Just an open conversation, whenever you need it.

Think of Seeker less like an app and more like that one friend who picks up at midnight, asks the right questions, and never makes you feel like a burden.

Voice-first

Just talk. Seeker listens and responds in a warm, natural voice — because typing how you feel at 2am is harder than it sounds.

Remembers you

Seeker builds a quiet understanding of who you are over time — your patterns, your wins, what weighs on you — so you never have to start from scratch.

Completely private

Everything stays on your device. Not stored on our servers, not processed in the cloud. What you share with Seeker stays with Seeker.

What Seeker can do

Everything you need.
Nothing you don’t.

Seeker isn’t trying to be a therapist, a productivity tool, or a wellness tracker. It’s just here — calm, available, and always on your side.

Talk, don’t type

Full on-device voice — your words are processed right on your phone, never sent anywhere. Speak naturally, like you would to a person. Seeker listens and responds out loud.

Here at 3am

Anxiety doesn’t keep office hours. Seeker is available on nights, weekends, and difficult Sundays — no appointment, no waitlist, no “I have a slot next Thursday.”

Grounding & breathwork

When things feel overwhelming, Seeker can walk you through breathing exercises and grounding techniques — small, science-backed tools that bring you back to yourself.

A gentle mood log

Seeker quietly tracks how you’re feeling over time. No charts to fill in — just a growing picture of your patterns, shared with you when it might actually help.

Crisis-aware, always

If Seeker detects signs of serious distress, it surfaces emergency helplines immediately — hardcoded, always on, no AI in the loop. A safety net that never fails.
