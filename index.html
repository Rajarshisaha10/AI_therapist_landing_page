Your privacy

What you tell Theraseek
stays with you.

We didn’t want to write another privacy policy and hope you trust it. So we built a system that physically cannot betray you — no policy required, because the architecture doesn’t allow it.

01
Your voice never leaves your phone

Speech-to-text and text-to-speech run entirely on-device. Your audio is never sent to a server — ours or anyone else’s.

02
No cloud AI processing

Theraseek runs a self-hosted, fine-tuned model locally on your device. There are no calls to OpenAI, no third-party APIs, no server-side logs.

03
We can’t read your data

If you choose to back up your conversations, they’re zero-knowledge encrypted. Even we cannot decrypt them. This is technical, not just policy.

04
Encrypted local memory

Theraseek’s understanding of you is stored encrypted on your phone. Backups for device migration are strictly opt-in—unless you choose otherwise, your data never leaves your device.

“Other apps promise privacy in a document. We built a system that makes betraying you technically impossible.”

Meet Theraseek

You don’t always need a therapist. Sometimes you just need someone to actually listen.

Theraseek is a voice-first AI companion built for those moments when things feel too heavy to carry alone — but you’re not ready (or able) to call someone. No appointments. No waitlists. No script. Just an open conversation, whenever you need it.

Think of Theraseek less like an app and more like that one friend who picks up at midnight, asks the right questions, and never makes you feel like a burden.

Voice-first

Just talk. Theraseek listens and responds in a warm, natural voice — because typing how you feel at 2am is harder than it sounds.

Remembers you

Theraseek builds a quiet understanding of who you are over time — your patterns, your wins, what weighs on you — so you never have to start from scratch.

Completely private

Everything stays on your device. Not stored on our servers, not processed in the cloud. What you share with Theraseek stays with Theraseek.
