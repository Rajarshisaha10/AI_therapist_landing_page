what makes it unique
Privacy-first with no trade-offs

Other apps promise privacy and cut corners elsewhere. We don't compromise on either — and our privacy isn't a policy, it's a system that physically can't leak.

on-device

Full on-device STT/TTS

Audio never leaves the phone. Whisper.cpp ensures no audio is sent, and Piper keeps audio entirely local.

self-hosted

Fine-tuned LLM

Not a generic API. Reverse proxy with no body logging, and LLM inference with absolutely no persistence.

sync

Zero-knowledge encrypted

Even we can't read user backups. Memory store is completely SQLCipher encrypted.

memory

Smart long-term memory

Persistent user profile with intelligent compaction, not just standard chat history.
